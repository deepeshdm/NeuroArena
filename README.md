# NeuroArena ⚔️🧠

**Where intelligence meets competition**

Think fast. Battle smarter. Know yourself.

NeuroArena is a real-time quiz battle platform that doesn't just test your knowledge—it analyzes your cognitive patterns. Compete against 9 other players in intense 10-question battles, then discover your unique cognitive profile, strengths, weaknesses, and personalized insights.

**What makes NeuroArena different?**
- 🔥 Real-time battles with WebSocket technology
- 🎯 10-player rooms, 10 questions, one winner
- 🧬 AI-powered personality profiling
- 📈 Actionable insights to improve your cognitive skills
- 🚀 Self-contained deployment (just one JAR!)

**Built with:** Spring Boot | WebSocket | Angular | MariaDB4j | JPA
